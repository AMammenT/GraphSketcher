Using the Info Popover

The Info popover provides style and property information about the graph or your selection. Here, you can design and arrange your graph to your heart’s content!
Tap the canvas to make sure nothing is selected. Then, tap the Info button in the tool bar to view the Canvas info.
Select a point, line, fill, label, or axis and tap the Info button to make adjustments to the selection.
