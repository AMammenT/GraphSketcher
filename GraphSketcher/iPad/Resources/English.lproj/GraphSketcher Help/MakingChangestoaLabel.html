Making Changes to a Label

Labels can be attached to the axes, points, lines, or just about anything! And if you like, they can also stand on their own. Double-tap an object or the canvas to add a label. To make changes to a label, tap the label and then tap the Info button in the toolbar. The Label Style popover appears with the following options:
1 — Change the label color by selecting a chit from the displayed palette or tap the rainbow chit on the right to see more color options.
2 — Tap to add a bold, italic, or underlined style to your label.
3 — Use the buttons to the left and right of the font size cell to change the font size. The button on the left decreases the size and the button on the right increases the size. Tap the cell in the center to type in a size.
4 — Tap the font family cell to view the list of font families. Choose a recommended font to ensure readability and commonality or, for fonts of all seasons, scroll to see the full list.
5 — Use the label coordinates to move the label to a precise location on the graph. Tap the cells to specify an x or y coordinate. The part of the label closest to the origin is the part that is moved to the new coordinates.
Unfortunately, Rich Text editing is currently unavailable so no subscripts or special characters for now.
